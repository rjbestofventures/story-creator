Voice Typing Guide
🎙️ Faster answers for longer responses

Speak Instead of Type

StoryCreator.Bot works best when your answers are thoughtful. When you have a longer answer, you can use Google Docs Voice Typing, then copy and paste the text into StoryCreator.Bot.

✓ Voice Typing Guide

i
Voice input is coming soon to StoryCreator.Bot.
Until then, Google Docs is a simple way to dictate your answers, especially when you do not want to type a long response.
1

Open a blank Google Doc

Go to docs.google.com/document and start a new blank document.

2

Turn on Voice Typing

From the Google Docs menu, select Tools, then choose Voice typing.

3

Click the microphone

A microphone box will appear on the left side of the screen. Click the microphone to begin.

4

Allow microphone access

If your browser asks for permission, select Allow while visiting this site.

5

Speak naturally

Start talking. Google Docs will turn your speech into text automatically.

  • ✓Speak clearly and pause between sentences.
  • ✓Do not worry about small mistakes. You can edit the text before pasting.
  • ✓Longer, more natural answers usually create better StoryCreator.Bot results.
6

Copy and paste into StoryCreator.Bot

When you are finished, copy the text from Google Docs and paste it into the StoryCreator.Bot answer box.

That’s it.

Use voice typing whenever an answer feels too long to type. Talk it out, clean it up, then paste it into StoryCreator.Bot.
